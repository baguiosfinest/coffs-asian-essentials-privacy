2. Camera

The app uses your device camera only to scan loyalty QR codes at the store counter. Camera images are processed on-device and are not stored, transmitted, or retained.

3. How we use your information

We use the information above solely to operate the loyalty programme, show your stamp balance and rewards, display store specials, and (if enabled) send you notifications. We do not sell your data, and we do not use advertising or tracking SDKs.

4. Remittance rates

The app displays publicly available AUD→PHP money-transfer rates (RIA, MoneyGram, Western Union) for information only. We are not affiliated with these providers and do not process any money transfer or collect financial information through the app.

5. How your data is stored

Account and loyalty data is stored securely using Supabase (a hosted Postgres database provider) and transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Access is restricted to authorised store staff.

6. Data sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except our infrastructure provider (Supabase) strictly to operate the app, or where required by law.

7. Data retention and deletion

We retain your loyalty data while your account is active. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. See Delete Your Account for the deletion process, or email us at the address below. We action deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Children

The app is intended for general store customers and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.
